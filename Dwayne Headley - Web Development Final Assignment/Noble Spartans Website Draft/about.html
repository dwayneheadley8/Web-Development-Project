Get to Know Us

All residents of the Timothy Greaves Dormitory are members of the illustrious Men's Club, a tight-knit committee comprising an executive board and diverse general body. The Men's Club is tasked with organizing and hosting events aimed at fostering the overall development of the dormitory's residents. Additionally, the Men's Club ensures that the voices of its residents are represented within the University's Administration Department and that all basic needs of the residents are adequately met.

President's Greetings

"I bid you welcome, fellow Spartan; I would like to invite you to the complex but warm atmosphere we call 'Timothy Greaves Dorm'. The Men's Club is an intriguing group of diverse and talented individuals who are dedicated to their craft. We value key principles such as unity, honesty, and respect. This is a community where our residents come together to create lasting memories, formidable friendships, learn from each other, and grow as individuals. I am honored to welcome you to the brotherhood."
